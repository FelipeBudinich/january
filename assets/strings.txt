January...


snow falls...


life fleets...


with or without you.



i hear your song...


though you do not speak.



January...


you are a revelation...



i find warm solitude


in your companionship.



when you are not present,


i am not present.



January...


you caught me by surprise


one spring,


just as you were leaving.



it was out of my control...


you had to go.



January...



January...



January...



let me count my thoughts,



all of which belong to you:
